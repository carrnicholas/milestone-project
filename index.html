A little about me...

I always have been interested in technology, since I was a little kid. But I wasn't always a developer. You could say that journey really started when I moved to Germany 10 days after I graduated from college. I found out by accident that I had a work permit and eventually landed a job at a software startup, partially becasue I spoke English natively, and partially because I had an iPhone. So I became the company's iOS software tester.

Over time, I taught myself the basics of coding and realized that I really enjoyed it. But I was using free resources to learn and didn't continuously dedicate myself to it. Eventually, I decided that if I was serious about learning to do this, I needed to invest money in a course. So I enrolled in Skillcrush's Break Into Tech blueprint. And now, here we are.

Who I Help

I help solopreneurs, small businesses, and individuals improve their online presence. I like websites with a minimalist feel, with color schemes that go together well. Easy on the eyes, and easy to read. That's my style.

I can code a simple website with HTML5 and CSS3 (the main languages that every website needs so you can see it on your computer), or I can build a site on Wordpress. The biggest advantage of Wordpress is that if you want to change the content, you can easily do this yourself. On a Wordpress site, your site will need onging security updates and maintenance. If you like, I can help with this ongoing maintenance for a monthly fee after your initial Wordpress site is done.
